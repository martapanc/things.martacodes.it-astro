Something I’d been wanting to do for years — ever since a coach tour across Spain and Portugal many years ago — I finally did a Balkan tour! 🎉

Over the course of a couple of weeks, the coach crossed 8 countries: Bulgaria, Serbia, Bosnia & Herzegovina, Croatia, Montenegro, Albania, North Macedonia, and Greece. So many beautiful cities and places, each with its own character.

My personal highlights? Sarajevo, with its fascinating mix of cultures and history; Lake Ohrid in North Macedonia, absolutely stunning; and the Meteora monasteries in Greece, perched on top of those dramatic rock formations — truly unforgettable 😍
