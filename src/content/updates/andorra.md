New country unlocked! 🎉 Andorra had been on my list for a while — it's one of those tiny European microstates that's easy to overlook, squeezed as it is between France and Spain in the Pyrenees. Went with a group of friends over a long weekend.

It's a surprisingly cozy place. The mountain scenery is beautiful, the old town of Andorra la Vella is charming, and yes — the duty-free shopping is very real (we may have stocked up on chocolate 😅). It's tiny enough to feel unhurried, which was exactly what we needed.

Country #... I've honestly lost count at this point, but it still feels special every time 🗺️
