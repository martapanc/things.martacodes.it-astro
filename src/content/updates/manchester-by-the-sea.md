Having lived in Manchester (the UK one, with the rain and the football) for several years, I found it mildly amusing to then fly to Massachusetts for our autumn team offsite and end up in… Manchester-by-the-Sea. Different vibe, I'll admit.

It's a gorgeous little coastal town north of Boston — proper New England charm, lobster rolls, wooden clapboard houses, the works. We spent a few days working together, which as always is a great reminder of how much easier things are when you're just in the same room.

Also managed a quick trip into Boston itself, which I hadn't visited before. Lovely city 🇺🇸
